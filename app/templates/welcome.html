The rules:

Rock paper scissors is usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously chooses:

  • Rock
  • Paper, or
  • Scissors

Possible results:

  • Rock crushes scissors
  • Paper covers rock
  • Scissors cuts paper

If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied and is usually immediately replayed to break the tie.
